[ NULL-BIT DITHER ]

An advanced, in-browser image dithering tool with a modern brutalist/cyberpunk theme. This project features multiple dithering algorithms (Floyd-Steinberg, Bayer, Stucki, etc.), a wide range of presets from retro Japanese computers and 8/16-bit consoles (PC-98, C64, MSX2), and expert-level fine-tuning controls for diffusion, gamma, saturation, and more.
